About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student majoring in data science at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen). I am also affiliated with the School of Science and Engineering of CUHK-Shenzhen as a research assistant. My main research interests include:

  • Federated learning

  • Gaussian processes

  • Signal processing

Currently, I am working with Zhidi Lin and Prof. Feng Yin on spectral kernel design for Gaussian processes.
