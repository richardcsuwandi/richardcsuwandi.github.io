Richard Cornelius Suwandi

Undergraduate Student & Research Assistant,

The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen)

I am a junior student majoring in Statistics (with concentration in Data Science) at the School of Data Science, CUHK-Shenzhen. I am also affiliated with CUHK-Shenzhen and Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data (SRIBD) as research assistants. I used to work with Prof. Tsung-Hui Chang and his PhD student, Mr. Shuai Wang, on federated matrix factorization. Recently, I have been working with Prof. Feng Yin and his PhD student, Mr. Zhidi Lin, on spectral kernel design for Gaussian processes.
