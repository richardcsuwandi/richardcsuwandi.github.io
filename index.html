Richard Cornelius Suwandi

Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen)

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Computer and Information Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen), under the supervision of Prof. Feng Yin and Prof. Tsung-Hui Chang. Prior to my PhD studies, I obtained my BSc degree in Statistics from CUHK-Shenzhen in 2023.

I am affiliated with the Bayesian Learning for Signal Processing (BLSP) Group of CUHK-Shenzhen, where my research mainly focuses on Bayesian learning, distributed optimization, and trustworthy machine learning.
